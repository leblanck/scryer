What is this?

This was created as a passion project becuase I constantly found myself checking multiple places for vocab/terms/slang/ etc used when playing Magic: The Gathering. I could not find a single place where I could easily search/filter/find words that I was reading and hearing in a quick way.

It is mosly vanilla HTML/CSS/JS with the exception of Handlebars.js for generating templating of the actual glossary data.

This website is OSS and can be found on GitHub. Please feel free to contribute to the addition of words or with any corrections you see that could be made.
